Yaya Toure

Gnégnéri Yaya Touré is an Ivorian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Chinese Super League club Qingdao Huanghai.

Yaya Toure has played 101 matches in his national team (Ivory Coast) and scored 19 goals.
